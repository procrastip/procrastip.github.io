How It Works

Procrastip helps you get things done. Whenever you are procrastinating, follow the steps below.

Step 1

Become aware you are procrastinating

This will take some practice, but over time you will make it a habit.

Step 2

Open the Procrastip guide

Open the website or an offline or printed version of the content. If this is your first time here, memorize procrastip.com and bookmark the page across your most used devices.

Step 3

Read and follow the practices

Be motivated to follow through and start achieving.

Step 4

Get things done

Complete the tasks you were supposed to be doing in the first place.

Step 5

Adjust the practices

Adjust the practices based on how they helped your performance in the past and your own preferences.

Step 6

Reinforce the habit

Over time, reinforce the habit by associating the procrastination trigger with Procrastip.
