About

Procrastip is a free website that aims to help you stop procrastination and get things done. It was founded with the intention of giving back to the community and the goal of having it evolve into something created by everyone to everyone, in line with the principles below.

Simplicity

The content you need to go back on track in the shortest and simplest possible format.

Ease of access

Easy-to-remember URL/domain. No clicks required to find the information you need. No login boxes. No paywalls.

Privacy protection

No registration required. No trackers. No ads.

Continuous improvement

Regular updates. Potentially crowd-sourced (if we are able to do so without disrespecting the other principles).


Author

My name is Luis Cinza and I am a Product Manager based in Luxembourg, Europe. I am passionate about exploring how one can be more effective and efficient for any type of goal and decided to share this guide with the strategies that worked for me over the years.
